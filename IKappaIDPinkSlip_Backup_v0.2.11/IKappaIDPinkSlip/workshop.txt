description=Me and Faded wanted to add a working PINKSLIP vehicles system to PZ B42.
description=
description=As there is still not one available, so why not being the first.
description=
description=This mod adds a full vehicle title system to Project Zomboid.
description=Instead of owning a vehicle only by world position, you can convert it into a physical Pink Slip item that holds the vehicle’s data, carry it in inventory, trade it, and redeploy it later.
description=
description=How It Works
description=Use a Blank Pink Slip to claim a nearby vehicle from the radial menu.
description=On claim, the vehicle is removed from the world and converted into a Filled Pink Slip.
description=The filled slip stores the captured vehicle state (script/type, part state, container contents, key-related data, and identity metadata).
description=Deploying the filled slip respawns the stored vehicle and consumes that exact slip.
description=The deployed vehicle keeps continuity through internal UID tagging so claim/deploy cycles remain traceable.
description=Main Gameplay Loop
description=Find or craft/acquire a Blank Pink Slip.
description=Stand near a vehicle and choose Claim Pink Slip.
description=Receive a filled title item in inventory.
description=Carry or trade the filled title item.
description=Use deploy action (hotkey/right-click/optional quick icon) to spawn the stored vehicle back into the world.
description=Ownership and Trading
description=Filled slips are normal inventory items, so they can be traded, stored, or looted.
description=Ownership can be reassigned via register flow when configured/used.
description=Optional server rule can require the registered owner to be the one who deploys.
description=Multiplayer / Server Behavior
description=The system is server-authoritative for important actions:
description=Claim validation
description=Deploy validation
description=Slip identity checks
description=The server resolves the slip from player inventory by UID rather than trusting client payload data.
description=This reduces abuse risk and keeps behavior consistent in MP.
description=Sandbox Configuration
description=Server admins can tune behavior with sandbox options such as:
description=
description=Enable/disable Pink Slip system
description=Max filled slips per player
description=Claim distance
description=Require empty vehicle for claiming
description=Require registered owner for deploy
description=Optional quick deploy icon visibility
description=Optional quick deploy icon movability
description=UI and Quality of Life
description=Claim action is integrated into vehicle radial menu.
description=Deploy is available through:
description=configurable keybind
description=filled-slip right-click action
description=optional left-side quick icon (can be hidden or moved)
description=Filled slip naming and tooltip readability were improved for easier inventory management.
description=Notes
description=Designed for Build 42 environment.
description=Supports singleplayer and multiplayer.
description=Best used on servers that want tradable, item-based vehicle ownership with admin-friendly controls.
